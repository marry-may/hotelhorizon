Marbella Club Hotel
Marbella, Spain
187 reviews

This elegant beachfront hotel, nestled between Marbella and Puerto Banús, boasts two stunning pools and five exceptional dining options. Surrounded by lush gardens with direct access to the beach, it offers an unparalleled experience. The Marbella Club Hotel, once the private residence of Prince Alfonso von Hohenlohe, the hotel’s founder, features rooms with private terraces offering breathtaking sea or garden views. Guests can enjoy a choice of five restaurants serving a blend of international and traditional Spanish cuisine.

The Hotel Botánico and Oriental Spa Garden 5*GL offers luxurious accommodations set amidst stunning gardens with views of the Atlantic Ocean and Mount Teide.
