Cookie policy

Hotel Horizon (“we,” “our,” or “us”) uses cookies to improve your browsing experience on our website. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. These files store information that helps us recognize your device and tailor your experience to your preferences. Cookies also enable us to analyze website performance, provide personalized content, and deliver targeted advertisements.

We use several types of cookies, each serving a specific purpose:

• Essential Cookies: These cookies are critical for the basic functionality of the website. They enable key features like secure login, navigation, and access to protected areas of the site. Without these cookies, certain parts of the website may not function properly.

• Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. They collect information about which pages are visited most frequently, how long users spend on our site, and any errors encountered. This data allows us to make improvements and ensure a seamless user experience.

• Functional Cookies: Functional cookies enable the website to remember choices you make, such as your language preference, region, or other customized settings. These cookies ensure a more personalized experience during your visit.

• Advertising Cookies: These cookies track your browsing activity and allow us to show you relevant advertisements based on your interests. They also help us measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and limit the number of times you see the same ad.

• Third-Party Cookies: Some cookies on our website are provided by trusted third-party services, such as analytics providers and advertising partners. These cookies help us gain insights into user behavior and improve the overall functionality of the site.

You can control how cookies are used through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to manage cookies by enabling, disabling, or deleting them as needed. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website, and some features may not work as intended.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to the placement of cookies on your device. If you wish to withdraw your consent, you can do so at any time by adjusting your browser settings or contacting us directly.

For more detailed information about our cookie practices or if you have any questions, please contact us at support@hotelhorizon.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we use cookies to enhance your experience.
